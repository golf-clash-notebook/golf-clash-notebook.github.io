Guide courtesy of Team Golf Clash Tommy.

Black Path

This is the most common way to play the hole, out to the right. If you get a terrible headwind, bounce it before the rough over to the fairway. That will leave you with a wood to the green. Bounce it before the rough/bunker and be aware that it's uphill. Don't use that much backspin!

White Path

Take this route when you get a good tailwind. Go all in and get it to the big fairway after the rough. From there you should be able to use a short iron into the green.

Yellow Path

A tricky way to play but if you're accurate enough you can bounce it over to the next fairway on the left. If you go long into the rough, you'll still be able to get to the green on your 2nd shot. Don't use max topspin on your drive as the second fairway is a bit downhill.
