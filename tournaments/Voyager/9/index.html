Guide courtesy of Team Golf Clash Tommy.

Black Path

The normal way to play the hole is to bounce your drive on the first fairway and over to the second. From there you will have a long way to the green so you'll need a wood with a lot of distance. Be aware that the trees on the right side of the fairway are in play so going too long on your drive will leave you with a more difficult second shot that will require curl. Also, leaving your approach short on the green will leave you with an impossible putt, so make sure you get it down towards the pin or leave it short for a chip.

White Path

This path is only an option if you get tailwind for your first shot and you're playing from one of the front 2 tees. Use some backspin to avoid running through the fairway. Your approach will be the same as the black path.
