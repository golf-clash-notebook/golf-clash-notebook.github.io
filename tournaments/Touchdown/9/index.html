Black Path

No matter the wind, the drive is just trying to get as far down the fairway as possible. With enough tailwind you can get your second shot over to the second fairway on the left. With a good shot, topspin and right sidespin you should be able to get the ball to the green. Making an eagle on this hole is not easy.

White Path

With headwind or sidewind, you won't be able to reach the second fairway with you second shot. In that case, keep playing up the right side and use a long iron with a good amount of backspin for your third shot. You can also use the rough just after the bunker and let your ball roll out to the pin.
