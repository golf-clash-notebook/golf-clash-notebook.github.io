Black Path

This is the typical way to play the hole unless you get good tailwind. Bounce over the bunker with some curl and max sidespin to the right. For the second shot a dunk is a very good option since the green will allow a missed dunk to fall back down to the hole for an easy putt.

White Path

If you get a good tailwind you can go directly over the bunker and kill it as far as possible on the fairway. That will leave you with a short iron or a wedge for your second shot and an excellent opportunity for an eagle.
